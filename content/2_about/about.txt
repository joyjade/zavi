Text: Zavi Kang Engles writes, walks, and tends to two cats in Tovaangar / Los Angeles. She is Managing Editor of (link: https://www.apogeejournal.org/ text: Apogee), a literary journal committed to thoughtful exploration of identity at its intersections. As a PhD student in the Department of American Studies and Ethnicity at the University of Southern California, she researches affect and embodiment in Asian American literature and Korean American shamanism.
