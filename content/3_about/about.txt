Zavi Kang Engles is a poet, writer, and researcher. Her most recent research project investigated far-right responses to the climate crisis. She is also managing editor elect of Apogee Journal. 

Zavi received her BA from Pitzer College, MA in Social Sciences from the *University of Chicago*, and a Fulbright ETA to Argentina. She is currently the Anne Waldman fellow at the Jack Kerouac School of Disembodied Poetics.
